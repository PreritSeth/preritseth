Starting the Cycle (Short Film)



Starting the Cycle is a short-film & PSA that was originally made for a TV Production 1 class for school. However, the full 1:30 video above was done as a personal project to explore videogrpahy, cinematography, and editing. I had a clear vision with this project and that was to make the final product look as developed and professional as possible. Using a Canon DSLR T2i to shoot, Sony Vegas Pro 13 to edit, and a couple of friends to act, I was able to self-produce this video.
