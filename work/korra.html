UltimateKorra Media Networks

    Branding & Media

UltimateKorra is a news blog and publishing network that I created at age 12, dedicated towards delivering the latest news and updates on the two popular Nickelodeon animated series: Avatar: The Last Airbender and The Legend of Korra. Having a passion for the television franchise, I thought what better way to express my interest than creating a news and updates blog.

Starting a blog based on something I was interested in fueled me to put forth my best efforts to make this project a success. Throughout the next few months, I explored social media, journalism, photo-editing, and business development. Several sleepless nights along with fierce execution allowed the website to gain significant exposure. As the initial blog yielded success, I thought it wouldn't be a bad idea to make a few more partner blogs, based on more specific topics. And thus, the UltimateKorra Media Network was born. Soon, The UltimateKorra Network became one of the leading distributors of Avatar and Korra related content in the community, with over 15,000 monthly readers and viewers. The network reigned at peak exposure in the summer of 2013 with over 70,000 hits in the month of July. The main Facebook page has almost 8,000 likes and the Twitter page has over 5,000 followers. The UltimateKorra YouTube page has over 3,000 subscribers and a total view count of over 1 million views!
